MBBS Duration and Medium of Instruction

The MBBS program at Kemerovo State Medical University is six years long, including academic learning and clinical internship. The course is offered in English medium for international students, which allows them to study medical subjects comfortably. However, students are also encouraged to learn basic Russian language skills as it helps during clinical practice and patient interaction in hospitals.

Eligibility Criteria and Admission Process

Students who wish to apply for MBBS admission must have completed their 10+2 education with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as core subjects. A minimum of 50 percent marks in PCB is generally required for general category students. Indian students must qualify NEET as per NMC guidelines to pursue MBBS abroad.

The admission process is simple and student-friendly. Applicants need to submit academic documents, passport copies, and application forms for evaluation. After document verification, the university issues an admission letter followed by an invitation letter for visa processing. Once the student visa is approved, students can travel to Russia and complete their enrollment at the university.
