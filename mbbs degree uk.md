<p><strong>What Is an MBBS Degree in the UK?</strong></p>

<p>
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">
    In the UK, the MBBS degree is commonly awarded as
  </span>
  <strong>MBBS, MBChB, or BMBS</strong>
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">
    , depending on the university. Despite the different names, all degrees are equivalent and recognized worldwide.
  </span>
</p>

<p>
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">
    Unlike some countries where MBBS takes 5.5 to 6 years, the
  </span>
  <a href="https://educationvibes.in/country/MBBS-Abroad/UK" rel="dofollow">
    <strong>MBBS degree in UK</strong>
  </a>
  <strong> is usually completed in 5 to 6 years</strong>
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">
    , including clinical training. Some universities also offer a
  </span>
  <strong>4-year graduate-entry medicine program</strong>
  <span style="font-weight: 400;">
    for students who already hold a relevant bachelor’s degree.
  </span>
</p>

<p><strong>Eligibility Criteria for MBBS Degree UK</strong></p>

<h3><strong>Academic Requirements</strong></h3>
<ul>
  <li>Completion of Class 12 with <strong>Biology, Chemistry, and Physics</strong></li>
  <li>High academic scores (usually 85–90% or above)</li>
  <li>Strong foundation in science subjects</li>
</ul>

<h3><strong>Entrance Exams</strong></h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>UCAT or BMAT</strong> (depending on the university)</li>
  <li>NEET qualification required for Indian students</li>
</ul>

<h3><strong>English Language Requirements</strong></h3>
<ul>
  <li>IELTS or equivalent English proficiency test</li>
