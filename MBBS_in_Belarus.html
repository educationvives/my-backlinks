Eligibility and Admission Process

To study MBBS in Belarus, Indian students must meet certain eligibility criteria. Students must have completed their higher secondary education with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as core subjects. A minimum percentage requirement may vary depending on the university, but most institutions require at least 50% marks in PCB subjects. Additionally, Indian students must qualify for the NEET examination to be eligible for medical education abroad according to NMC guidelines.

The admission process typically involves submitting academic documents, passport copies, and application forms to the chosen university. After receiving the admission letter, students apply for a student visa and complete the necessary travel arrangements before joining the university.
